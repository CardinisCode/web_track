Pretty Fly (For A White Guy) Want You Bad (Can't Get My) Head Around You Can't Repeat The Kids Aren't Alright

Just listening to this now brings back memories of my first year in College. Dated a guy towards the end of that college year and I ended it shortly after I moved to London. We tried to continue things long distance but there's only so much you can do over sms. It was only a few years later that I was introduced to the Hotmail chat functionality, which actually included video chat LOL. He's a great guy, he was a great partner and this music definitely brings back memories we spent together. Brings a smile to my face :) Good times!
