About Andi

I am Andrea Folgado, from Cape Town, South Africa. I have been living in London for the past year and I have spent the majority of this year studying. I moved to the UK as I would love to travel around the UK and Europe, and to spend more time with my family in the UK, Portugal and Switzerland. However, above all I would love to see my grandmother in Portugal and spend some time with her person. My partner and I had plans to visit Portugal earlier this year but had to cancel due to Covid. Our plans to travel back to South Africa over Christmas have sadly also been put on hold. Since it's hard to plan with so many unpredictable variables, it's very likely we'll be spending the Christmas holidays in our apartment. I seriously hope things don't go that way though.

Although I don't believe in the reasons behind Christmas and it's pretty much a pagan holiday, I do love the opportunity to spend time with family. I'm more than happy to exhange gifts with family and friends, as it's an opportunity to bless those you care about.
