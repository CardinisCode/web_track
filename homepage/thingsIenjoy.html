There are a few things I enjoy about this game:

* As you play the various game scenarios, you're taken through history itself * You're given a limited hand of options each round, which encourages you to strategize whilst simultaneously narrowing the options to prevent analysis paralysis * The element of luck highlights the challenges of war, where good strategies don't always lead to the intended results * Due to the nature of the game, you have to think on your feet in the moment and do the best you can with what you've got * You don't need to build & paint the models before you can play a game
